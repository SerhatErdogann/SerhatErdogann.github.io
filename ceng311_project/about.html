About Us

Our Story

Following the devastating earthquake on February 6, university students in Ankara came together to launch the “Toy Inventors” project. Their goal was to bring a little joy to the lives of the affected children and offer them moral support. Leveraging a variety of crafting techniques and materials, the team produced colorful and imaginative toys. This process not only helped the young participants develop their manual skills and enrich their creativity, but also united them in a shared act of social responsibility. Beyond simply providing gifts, each toy was intended to serve as a comforting companion, aiding children in their healing process. In this way, “Toy Inventors” evolved into a movement emphasizing empathy, solidarity, and creativity, all while aiming to make a meaningful impact on the community.

Vision

To pioneer a future where social solidarity and creativity are strengthened by producing toys that enrich the imagination of children—especially those affected by disasters—and provide emotional support. By creating a safe and enjoyable learning environment, the project contributes to children's well-being while helping young people develop their skills and embrace a sense of social responsibility.

Mission

To address the emotional and psychological needs of children affected by the earthquake with hand-made, thoughtfully designed toys. To provide university students and young people with a creative space where they can hone their manual skills and discover their potential while fostering a sense of social responsibility. To support children’s emotional recovery by offering specially designed toys that help them process traumatic experiences, thereby contributing to their overall rehabilitation.
